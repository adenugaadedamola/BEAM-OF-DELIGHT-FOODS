About Beam of Delight

Beam of Delight Foods is a newly established business in Regina, dedicated to offering healthy, freshly baked foods tailored to individual preferences. Previously operating in Nigeria under the name Beam of Delight Limited, we gained experience in producing high-quality products such as Milk Bread, Coconut Bread, Sardine Bread, Wheat Bread, Chinchin, Cupcakes, and more. Our specialty lies in providing chemical-free, customizable baked foods, natural drinks and delivering exceptional service with guaranteed satisfaction.
