An important note on allergens.

Whilst we do offer foods to suit different allergies, it's essential to note that we do not run a "free from" kitchen.
We take care to ensure that cross contamination is avoided, however cannot guarantee that our products won't come into contact with milk, wheat,soy, and other allergens.

Our bread is not made to last for weeks as they are without improver. They should be refrigerated, and special breads can be made with or without yeast based on customer’s request. For long-term storage, keep the bread in the freezer if not consumed within a week.
